Running BuzzRef on macOS

When you first open BuzzRef on macOS, you may see a security warning because the app is from an unidentified developer. Follow these steps to run it:

1

Close the Error Dialog

Click OK to close the initial warning dialog.

2

Open System Settings

Go to Apple menu - System Settings (or System Preferences on older macOS).

3

Go to Privacy & Security

Navigate to Privacy & Security settings.

4

Allow the App

Look for a message about BuzzRef being blocked. Click 'Open Anyway'.

5

Reopen BuzzRef

Double-click BuzzRef.app again to launch it.

6

Confirm

Click 'Open' in the confirmation dialog.

This authorization is only required once. After this, BuzzRef will open normally.
